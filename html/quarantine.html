Q U A R A N T I N E

Rainbow Seven Quarantine is an upcoming squad based tactical shooter and the fourteenth installment in the Rainbow Seven Franchise. The game is set several years in the future and tasks a three-player team of Operators to work together and fight off the increasing threat of a technological parasite. The game was announced on June 6, 2019 at E3 2019.

The game will be a three-player co-op shooter, encouraging teamwork as you fight against alien monsters rather than each other. The action, weapons, and gadgets are similar to previous entry Rainbow Seven Siege, though of course with a horror twist.

Rainbow Seven Quarantine Release Date

there is no official release date for the game as of yet. a Ubipost financial call, CEO Yves Guillemot revealed that the game will be released before the end of the current fiscal year. so, Rainbow Seven Quarantine will be released in 2020 but there is a possibility that this time could be extended until March 2021.
