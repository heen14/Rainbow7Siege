SEASONS UPDATES

In Rainbow Seven Siege, Seasons are major game updates that are focused on improving the experience. Every Season we release new free content including maps, features or game modes as well as new unlockable content including operators, weapon or character skins.

2020

OPERATION STEEL WAVE

RELEASE DATE : JUNE 2020

Don't miss a beat with Operation Steel Wave. Discover the new hard-breacher Ace and his S.EL.M.A Aqua Breacher that combines the demolitionist’s explosive force with the humanitarian’s care. On the other side of the action, don’t be too alarmed if you find yourself slowed when there’s no barbed wire underfoot. This is the Banshee Sonic Defence that will alert Melusi of your presence. With them, expect a newly reworked House coming into the game.

New Operators + New Maps + Seasonal skin

OPERATION VOID EDGE

RELEASE DATE : MARCH 2020

For the second year of Rainbow 7 Siege, give some space to our two new operators as they break into this season with their unique abilities. Thanks to her Gemini Replicator, attacker Iana can create a usable clone of herself, while Defender Oryx can use his Remah Dash against enemies and walls.

New Operators + Map Rework + Seasonal skin + Operators Balance

2019

OPERATION PARA BELLUM

RELEASE DATE : DECEMBER 2019

In Operation Para Bellum, two G.I.S. Operators thrive under the blaze of the Tuscan sun. Team Rainbow is deploying Maestro and Alibi in the villa of one of the most dangerous crime families.

New Operators + New Map + Weapons skin + Operators Balance

OPERATION BLOOD ORCHID

RELEASE DATE : SEPTEMBER 2019

In Operation Blood Orchid, two Special Duties Unit Operators join Team Rainbow to raid Theme Park. This season will showcase a reworking of the Operators and maps following an optimization of our data, namely a rework of textures, polished dynamic and static lighting, richer sky domes, new customization, and much more.

New Operators + New Maps + New Weapon

OPERATION VELVET SHELL

RELEASE DATE : JUNE 2019

In Operation Velvet Shell, two G.E.O. Operators join Team Rainbow to regain control of the "Coastline". Following a year of post-launch content, this major update aims to improve player comfort with an upgraded navigation interface, customisation selections and lots more.

New Operators + New Maps + New Weapon

OPERATION GRIM SKY

RELEASE DATE : MARCH 2019

For Operation Grim Sky, Rainbow Seven is deploying Clash and Maverick. Experts in mob behavior and high-precision strikes respectively, they'll also assess the new training facilities at the reworked Hereford Base. Out with the old and in with the new.

New Operators + Map Rework + Weapon Skins + Map Buff
